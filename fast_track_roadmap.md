# 6-Day Fast-Track: WebGame

Accelerated development plan for a portfolio-ready launch.

## 6-Day Plan

### Day 1: The Local Sandbox (Completed)
- Engine, isometric camera, and trigonometric WASD movement.

### Day 2: The Procedural Solar System (Completed)
- Finalize `World.js`, procedural planets, and starry environment.
- **Status:** Expanded to 8 planets, asteroid belt, and bloom post-processing.

### Day 3: The Multiplayer Handshake (Next)
- Node.js/Socket.io backend for real-time player position syncing.

### Day 4: Combat and Collision
- Shooting (`Spacebar`) and hit registration logic.

### Day 5: Interface and Polish
- HTML/CSS HUD for health and kill counters.

### Day 6: Live Deployment
- Frontend hosting on **Vercel** and backend on **Railway**.
